Meaning of FEEW Mindmeld

Mindmeld typically refers to a deep, intuitive connection or fusion of thought, ideas, or mental states between two or more people. It implies a seamless sharing of understanding, perspective, or creativity — as if minds are "melded" together.

The term is often used metaphorically ("we had a mindmeld during the brainstorming session") and in science fiction (for example, Star Trek). It can also refer to a literal telepathic merging of minds.
